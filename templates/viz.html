This is an interactive visualization of the retrieved search results.

Click on a multimedia documents group (orange colored), multimedia document (pink colored) or multimedia snippet (blue colored) to move browse the contents associate with it.

The selected item will be centered and contents of the selected item will be displayed on the right panel.

Use the mouse wheel to zoom and drag and drop the canvas to pan.
